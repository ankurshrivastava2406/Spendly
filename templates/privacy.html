Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and power filtering features. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all associated data from our systems.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but these providers do not have access to your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the support contact on our website. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly.
